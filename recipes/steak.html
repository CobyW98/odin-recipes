Steak

This recipe makes one delicious, juicy, steak, cooked medium rare, the way god intended it. As humanity continues to rein supreme over the grass eaters, just remember cows have feelings too. Bone apetit!

Ingredients

  • Steak (duh!)
  • Salt and pepper. We like flavor on this website
  • Enough butter to clog an elephant's aortic valve

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan over medium high heat
  2. Throw some olive oil on that joint
  3. When the oil can move visuously about the pan, and is on the verge of smoking (but not quite smoking), throw the stake on
  4. Let it cook for about 3 minutes, forming a nice crust
  5. Flip the steak, cook for an additional 3 minutes
  6. Put the butter in the pan. Don't be afraid to go overboard. There's no such thing as too much butter.
  7. Cook for another mintue, basting the steak with the butter.
  8. Remove the steak and let that baby sit for five minutes.
  9. Eat that steak. And with every bite just remember, nobody loves you, you're destined to die alone
