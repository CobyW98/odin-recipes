Delicious Lasagna

This is a recipe for delicious lasagna (some call it the best in the world). This recipe has been passed down through generations of Italian grandmothers and mafiosos. Even though lasagna is not an Italian dish. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Ragu extra meat sauce
  • Parmiggiano romano
  • Dry lasagna sheets
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. With a big baking dish, carefully make three layers, each one with equal amounts of lasagna, meat, and cheese
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees farenheit. Hot enough to melt away all your insecurities.
  3. Put the tray in the oven and wait for 40 minutes.
  4. Congratulations, you just made lasagna. Yummy!
